Hotel and Restaurants

Experience exceptional hospitality and culinary delights with our curated selection of hotels and restaurants. From cozy retreats to gourmet dining, we offer memorable experiences tailored to your preferences. Book your stay and dining reservations now with PARADISE for an unforgettable journey.

Michelin Star (Paris)

The restaurant’s philosophy is based on the notion of ‘Essential Cuisine’ to retain the natural and authentic flavours of every dish. The signature delicacies include smoked yoghurt, smoked beef, and grilled silk grain veal amongst others.

Taj Mahal Palace (Mumbai)

The Taj Mahal Palace, Mumbai, a historic icon since 1903, blends art and architecture seamlessly. Admire Belgian chandeliers and Indian masterpieces in its diverse interiors. Wander its corridors, then relax in rooms with serene views. Indulge in holistic treatments at the Jiva Spa by the pool for a rejuvenating experience.

Taj Falaknuma Palace (Hyderabad)

Taj Falaknuma Palace, once the Nizam of Hyderabad's royal abode, now offers guests a regal experience in its restored setting. Richly adorned guestrooms, Rajasthani Garden strolls, and Jiva Spa indulgences await. Dining choices include fine Italian cuisine, garden feasts, and Adaa, an Indian-Hyderabadi fine dining gem.

Avartana (Chennai)

ITC Grand Chola, with its verdant surroundings and modern amenities, including a spa and pool, features 11 dining spaces. Avartana, a standout among these, offers a blend of traditional and contemporary Southern Indian cuisine. Don't miss their innovative dishes like Infused Tomato Rasam and Pan Fried Cod.

Rim Naam (Bengaluru)

Nestled in Bengaluru's Garden City, The Oberoi, Bengaluru, is a haven for business travelers seeking efficiency and luxury. With state-of-the-art IT amenities and four dining options, including the exquisite Rim Naam, guests can seamlessly balance work and leisure amidst serene surroundings.

Masala Bay (Mumbai)

Taj Lands End, located on Bandra Bandstand, boasts stunning views of Mumbai's skyline, Bandra fort, and the Arabian Sea. Its proximity to airports and modern amenities cater to business travelers. Masala Bay offers a luxurious dining experience with traditional Indian cuisine reinvented with a modern touch.
